ZOO MANAGEMENT APPLICATION

Employee Parking System

The employees of the zoo are pre-allocated parking spaces based on availability and type of vehicle. These slots can change on a daily basis, and the employee can decide whether they will bring in their vehicle on a particular day and book a spot accordingly. We can have one separate database for this

Fee entrance system

This is accessible to the employee at the front desk of the zoo, who manages ticket sales based on the number of adult and children guests. The entry fees to the zoo can be modified according to the season, the day of the week, and the ages. The name of the lead of a pack can be obtained to be filled in a second database.

Employee maintenance system

All the employees of the zoo can be maintained, which can include their shifts, their off days, the animals they take care of, and their salary. This can be filled in a third database.

Animal vaccination system

All the vaccination details pertaining to the animals of the zoo can be fed into the microservice so that the zookeepers can be alerted about their next vaccination schedule. This can be compiled in a fourth database, along with a relation to the employee database

Based on the general animalsâ€™ eating habits, a feeding schedule can be maintained on the system. Later on, if specific animals have a special diet, their feeding schedule can be personalized accordingly. This can be in the same database as the animal vaccination system.
